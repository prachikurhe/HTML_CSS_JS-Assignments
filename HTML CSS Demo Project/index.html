About Pune:

Pune is the second-largest city in the state of Maharashtra after Mumbai, and is an important city in terms of its economic and industrial growth. Once the centre of power for the Maratha Empire, Pune’s rich historical past has made it the cultural capital of Maharashtra.





Best place to vist in Pune is Lonawala:

Situated in the Sahyadri range of the Western Ghats close to Pune and Mumbai, Lonavala is the most visited hill station in Maharashtra and the place to be during monsoons.With lots of waterfalls, lakes and hills around, it is a popular destination for camping, trekking and other adventure sports.Adjacent to Lonavala, Khandala is a popular hill station near Lonavala, which is located in the western ghats of Maharashtra. The main reasons to make Khandala a popular hill station near Mumbai are beautiful points like Amrutanjan Point, Duke's Nose, Bhushi's Lake, and Tiger's Leap.
